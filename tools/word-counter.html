What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a tool that calculates the number of words and characters in a piece of text. It is widely used by students, writers, bloggers, and professionals to meet writing limits and improve content quality.

Why Use a Word Counter?

Word limits are important for essays, assignments, articles, and SEO content. This tool helps ensure your writing meets the required length while maintaining clarity and structure.

How to Use This Tool

Simply paste or type your content into the text box and click “Count Text.” The tool will instantly display the number of words and characters.

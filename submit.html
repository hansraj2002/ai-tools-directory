Submit an AI Tool for Review

AI Tools Directory accepts submissions from developers, startups, and creators who want to showcase useful artificial intelligence tools. All submissions are reviewed manually to ensure they provide genuine value to users before being listed in the directory.

Submitting a tool does not guarantee approval. Our editorial process focuses on relevance, usability, originality, and transparency. We aim to maintain a high-quality directory that helps visitors discover practical and trustworthy AI solutions.

How the Review Process Works

  • ✔ Submitted tools are reviewed by a human editor
  • ✔ We verify the tool’s functionality and public availability
  • ✔ We check descriptions for clarity and accuracy
  • ✔ Low-quality, misleading, or duplicate tools are rejected

We do not accept automated submissions, spam, or misleading promotions. Tools that violate laws, misuse user data, or offer deceptive claims will not be listed.

Important Disclosure

Submitting a tool is free. We do not charge for standard listings and do not sell rankings or guaranteed placements. Any advertising displayed on the website is clearly separated from editorial content and does not influence which tools are featured.

Why Submit Your AI Tool?

  • Reach users actively searching for AI solutions
  • Gain visibility among developers, students, and businesses
  • Be part of a growing, curated AI tools directory
